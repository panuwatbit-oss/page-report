Privacy Policy for Leo Messenger Read Only

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Leo Messenger Read Only is an internal business tool used by authorized administrators to manage marketing reports, Facebook Page content workflows, and Page-related operational checks for pages they own or manage.

Information We Access

When an authorized administrator connects a Meta account or Page, the tool may access Page identifiers, Page names, Page access permissions, Page post metadata, Page engagement data, Messenger conversation metadata required for analysis, and advertising or business asset information that the administrator has permission to use.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to provide requested business functions, including reporting, Page content preparation, approved Page publishing, Messenger analysis, and troubleshooting access or permission issues.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share Meta user or Page data with third parties except service providers required to operate the internal system, or when required by law.

Data Retention

Operational records and reports are retained only as long as needed for business, audit, troubleshooting, and client reporting purposes. Access tokens and credentials are stored securely and are not published in reports or public pages.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of data associated with this tool, contact the system owner at panuwat.bit@gmail.com. We will review and delete eligible records that are no longer required for business, legal, or security purposes.

Contact
